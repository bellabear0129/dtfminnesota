Artwork

File requirements

📄
PNG format, transparent background
All artwork must be submitted as PNG with a transparent (not white) background for accurate color and edge definition.
🔍
300 DPI minimum
We require a minimum of 300 DPI at print size. Low-res files will be flagged before production — we won't print bad artwork.
🎨
RGB color mode
Submit files in RGB. Our RIP software handles color conversion. CMYK submissions may shift unexpectedly.
📐
Gang sheets: 22" max width
Gang sheet files must be sized to exactly 22" wide at 300 DPI. Height can be any length. Include 0.25" bleed between designs.
⚠️
You own your artwork
As a wholesale supplier, we print what you send. You are responsible for copyright clearance and print-ready quality.

Common questions

FAQ

Do you have order minimums?
No. You can order as little as a single transfer. Pricing simply scales with volume — the more you order, the better your per-unit rate.
Do you offer wholesale accounts with net terms?
Yes. Shops that maintain consistent monthly volume can apply for a wholesale account with net 30 terms. Contact us to discuss your volume needs.
What if there's a problem with my order?
If we made a production error, we reprint it — simple. We do not rerun orders due to low-resolution artwork or incorrect file setup, so please follow file requirements carefully.
Can I upload a pre-built gang sheet?
Yes. We accept pre-built gang sheets at 22" wide × any length, 300 DPI, PNG with transparent background. You can also upload individual designs and we'll arrange them on the sheet for you.
What fabrics do DTF transfers work on?
DTF transfers adhere to cotton, polyester, blends, nylon, leather, and most other fabrics. They work on any color garment, unlike sublimation which requires white or light polyester.
How do I submit files and pay?
Use our Shop page to upload files, select your options, and pay online. For large or custom orders, contact us first and we'll send you a custom quote.
