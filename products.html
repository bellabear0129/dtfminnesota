Artwork

File requirements

PNG, transparent background
All artwork must be PNG with a transparent (not white) background for accurate color and edge definition.
300 DPI minimum
We require 300 DPI at print size. Low-res files will be flagged — we won't print bad artwork.
RGB color mode
Submit files in RGB. Our RIP handles conversion. CMYK submissions may shift unexpectedly.
Gang sheets: 22" max width
Gang sheet files must be 22" wide at 300 DPI. Include 0.25" bleed between designs.
You own your artwork
You are responsible for copyright clearance and print-ready quality.

Common questions

FAQ

Do you have order minimums?
No. You can order as little as a single transfer. Pricing simply scales with volume.
Do you offer wholesale accounts with net terms?
Yes. Shops with consistent monthly volume can apply for a wholesale account with net 30 terms. Contact us to discuss.
What if there's a problem with my order?
If we made a production error, we reprint it — simple. We do not rerun orders due to low-resolution artwork or incorrect file setup.
Can I upload a pre-built gang sheet?
Yes. We accept pre-built sheets at 22" wide, 300 DPI, PNG with transparent background. You can also upload individual designs and we'll arrange them.
What fabrics do DTF transfers work on?
Cotton, polyester, blends, nylon, leather, and most other fabrics. Any color garment — unlike sublimation which requires white polyester.
