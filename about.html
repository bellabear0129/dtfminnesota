Who we are

Born from the print shop floor.

DTF Minnesota was started by people who spent years working inside print shops. We know what it feels like to have a client deadline breathing down your neck, a supplier that's two days late, and a transfer that won't press right.

We wholesale exclusively — meaning every dollar, every workflow, every decision is built around the needs of professional print shops. Not hobbyists. Not Etsy sellers. Print shops.

Based in Brooklyn Park, MN, we produce in-house on commercial DTF equipment. We control quality at every step, turn around orders fast, and when something goes wrong we fix it — no runaround.

How we work

Our production process

01

File intake

You upload print-ready PNG files. We check resolution, transparency, and sizing before queuing.

02

Print & cure

Printed on DTF film with our 9-color system, then powdered and cured for adhesion.

03

Quality check

Every order inspected before packaging. Color, coverage, cut lines — verified by hand.

04

Ship same day

Orders in before noon ship same day. Tracking sent automatically.
